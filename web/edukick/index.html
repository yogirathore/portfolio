Programs

What We Offer.

Train Young

The Trainyoung program is for children from ages 8 to 16, and it ensures that education is important at all times. We ensure that students can concentrate on their studies while playing.

At Trainyoung, we believe that education is the key to helping our children grow up and become successful.

Overview

BePro

BePro program is for children of age 16 and above, providing advanced coaching for those who wish to compete at a national level or advance their education towards their desired profession.

The program focuses on academic excellence with a mix of sport and exercise activities that will develop the body, mind and soul of each child
